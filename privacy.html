Privacy Policy

My Philosophy

I built The Steady Hand (TSH) as a private sanctuary for you. I don't track you, I don't use accounts, and I have no interest in your personal data. This app is a tool for your growth, not for my collection.

How I Store Your Data

Everything important—your habits, your streaks, and your voice recordings—stays only on your device. I use LocalStorage and IndexedDB to keep this data local to you. I have zero access to it.

The Wall of Wisdom

When you post a message to the Wall, it is sent to my server so others can read it. While these posts are anonymous, your IP address is processed during the transmission. I do not link this to your local data in any way.

Third-Party Services

I use a few standard services (Cloudflare, Google, Lucide) to make sure the app loads quickly and looks right. These providers may see your basic connection info when you open the app.
